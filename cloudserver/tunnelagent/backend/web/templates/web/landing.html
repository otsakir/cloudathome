CloudAtHome

CloudAtHome lets you expose services running on your home network to the internet — without opening firewall ports or using a VPN.

It works by establishing a secure SSH reverse tunnel from your home machine to a cloud server. Incoming HTTPS requests are routed by hostname through the tunnel directly to your home service.
